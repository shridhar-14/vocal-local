🛍️ **Vocal for Local A Full-Stack MERN E-Commerce Platform Supporting Local Businesses**

📌 **Project Overview**

**Vocal for Local** is a full-stack e-commerce web application built using the **MERN Stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js)**. The platform is designed to promote and empower local vendors and small businesses by providing them with an online marketplace to showcase and sell their products. Customers can browse, search, and purchase locally made products easily through a modern and user-friendly interface.

🚀 **Features**

👩‍💼 **Vendor Features**

--Vendor registration & login

--Add, update, and delete products

--Manage product listings

🛒 **Customer Features**

--User authentication (Signup/Login)

--Browse products by category

--Search and filter products

🔐 **Authentication & Security**

--JWT-based authentication

--Protected routes

--Password hashing
